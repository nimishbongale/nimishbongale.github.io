Rucio WebUI (CERN GSoC)

Contributed to CERN's Rucio ecosystem by transforming and re-architecting the WebUI during Google Summer of Code, collaborating with scientists from ATLAS and CMS workflows.

Problem

Scientific data workflows require robust and intuitive interfaces for petabyte-scale data movement and management. Existing UX and architecture needed modernization to better support experiment teams.

Approach

  • Collaborated with maintainers and scientific users to map high-priority WebUI improvements.
  • Re-architected key UI flows for better maintainability and operational clarity.
  • Aligned implementation with open-source standards and existing Rucio ecosystem constraints.
  • Produced technical implementation notes and usage guidance for maintainers and users.
  • Delivered production-ready contributions within a four-month GSoC timeline.
