Problem

Teams needed reliable AI assistance for day-to-day productivity, but existing prototypes struggled with consistency, enterprise constraints, and deployment-readiness for high-scale usage.

Approach

  • Designed a multimodal agent stack integrating text, speech-to-text, and text-to-speech capabilities.
  • Combined retrieval and context augmentation patterns (RAG/CAG) to improve task grounding quality.
  • Implemented secure access patterns and tenant-aware controls for enterprise AI usage.
  • Implemented enterprise prompting techniques including Chain-of-Thought-inspired workflow decomposition.
  • Integrated a patent-pending micro-frontend desktop experience for seamless user adoption.
